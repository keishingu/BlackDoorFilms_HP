About Our Team

Black Door Films is a climbing-focused video team based at the foot of Mt.Kaikomagatake.

We’re Climber

We are all climbers with more than 10 years of mountaineering experience and are good at ropework and altitude work.

Based at Hokuto and Fujimi

Our team is based at the foot of Mt. Komagatake and has access to Yatsugatake, the Japanese Alps and Mt. Mizugaki within one hour.

Why we do it

Until now, most climbing footage in Japan has been record. We aim to create works with high artistic value.
